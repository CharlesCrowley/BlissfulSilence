Blissful Silence, Come!

Contemplative Poetry, Journaling and Music.

Ending Pretending

Ending pretending;
swallowing its pride
admitted ignorance;
It’s not meant to guide.

Release its burden;
don't be unkind.
It's all too much
for this little mind.

It promoted itself 
into redundancy.
Give it a new title:
head of vacancy.

Made to be a servant
not a mighty King.
Ruling over all matters
was never its thing.

Let it do its job—
to see happening.
Not the why and how
of most everything.

To my mind I say, “Enjoy!
take some of this.”
Surrender your trying
and taste pure bliss.

God Is

God is you.
God is me.
God is everything
you see.

 

Stop.
Breathe. Just be.
Never were you
not free.

Mi Pobre Orgullo

¿Qué lugar tiene mi orgullo
si solo comprendí
dejándome ir?

 

Too Easy

Give me the solution!
But make it complicated
for my mind’s sake.

 

If it’s too simple,
I might actually have to
put it into practice.

To Live Itself

Oh mind!

 

If you don’t get what you want
you are miserable.
When you do get it there is a brief
moment of joy.
Once you have had it for a while
you get bored,
or you start worrying about losing it
or set off to chase the next goal.

 

What a torture!

 

Since nothing will ever satisfy you…
Why not let life live itself through you,
and enjoy the unfolding in peace?

Silence

Not merely noise in absence
but attention in presence.

Not You

It’s not you. It’s not you.
That’s a voice in your head.

 

It’s not you. It’s not you.
By the blind you are led.

 

It's not you. It's not you.
Follow your heart instead.
